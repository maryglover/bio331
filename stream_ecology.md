## Learning objectives

- Discuss the ecosystem and economic services of freshwater ecosystems 
- Define types of freshwater ecosystems, including rivers, lakes, and wetlands
- Define a watershed
- Describe the properties that characterize aquatic habitats. 
- Describe the different zones of a river
- Explain how human activities threaten freshwater ecosystems
- Discuss the impacts of urbanization on nearby streams and rivers
- Describe the role of riparian buffers

In this class, we will:

1. Discuss the basics of freshwater ecosystems.
1. Review the structure of introduction sections in scientific papers
1. Review the [Base R lesson](../baseR/BaseR.html) and finish the airplane data activity
